# Personas - Antique Bookstore

## Overview

This document defines representative user personas for the internal Antique Bookstore system.
Personas are based on the business scenario, operational environment, and identified user roles.

The system is used exclusively by staff; customers do not interact directly with it.

## Persona 1 - Store Manager

Role: Manager
Age: 65
Experience: 30+ years in rare book retail
Technical proficiency: Low; uses desktop applications rarely

Goals:

- Maintain accurate inventory of rare and antiquarian books
- Oversee all orders, payments, and deliveries
- Manage employees, positions, and access levels
- Ensure the system reflects the real state of the store

Pain points:

- Paper-based records lead to errors
- Difficult to track sold vs. available copies
- Hard to supervise order status and employee performance
- Time-consuming manual cross-checks

Needs:

- Full read/write access to books, authors, customers, employees, orders
- Clear visibility into inventory and orders
- Ability to correct mistakes and update statuses instantly
- Reliable audit trail for all changes

## Persona 3 - Sales Representative 

Role: Salesperson
Age: 29
Experience: 3 years retail
Technical proficiency: Medium; prefers simple workflows

Goals:

- Create orders quickly during customer interactions
- Check book availability instantly
- Register payments efficiently
- Provide accurate answers about authors, prices, and book conditions

Pain points:

- Slow lookups in paper catalogs
- Confusing paper order forms
- Risk of selling a book that is already reserved or sold
- Manual mistakes in copying book IDs and statuses

Needs:

- Ability to search books fast by title/author
- A simple order-creation workflow
- Clear status indicators (Available / Reserved / Sold)
- Restricted but sufficient permissions to modify order data
